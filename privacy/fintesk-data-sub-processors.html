Fintesk Data Sub-processors

Status: Current as of November 27, 2025.

Important Notice: The original version of this document was written in Spanish. Any other version available in other languages should be considered a translation. In case of discrepancy, the provisions of the Spanish version shall prevail.

Introduction​

Fintesk may use sub-processors to support the provision of services ordered by a Client. This page provides important information about the identity, location, and role of each sub-processor, as well as the type of data they process.

1. Third-Party Sub-processors​

Infrastructure and Hosting​

These providers are fundamental to the basic operation and security of the platform.

Sub-processorEntity CountryTransfer MechanismApplicable ServiceType of Personal Data
DigitalOcean LLCUSADPAHosting and CDN services in Europe and the USA (depending on Client location).All Client Data stored, as defined in the Terms of Service.
Cloudflare, Inc.USADPAContent distribution, security, abuse prevention, and DNS services.End-User or Agent IP address.

Feature Specific Providers​

These providers enable optional or specific features within Fintesk.

Sub-processorEntity CountryTransfer MechanismApplicable ServiceType of Personal Data
Messagebird UK Ltd.United KingdomDPAProvider for the Live Chat feature in LeadBooster.Encrypted conversations sent through the Live Chat feature.
Google LLCUSADPAProvider for AI features (email summarization, generation, data analysis).All Client Data that the Client chooses to process in AI-powered features.
Mailgun Technologies, IncUSADPAProvider for the Simple Email featureEmails sent using the feature.

Customer Support Services​

Sub-processors involved when you request technical support.

Sub-processorEntity CountryTransfer MechanismApplicable ServiceType of Personal Data
Splashtop Inc.USADPAScreen sharing and co-browsing to resolve issues.Data that the Client chooses to share during screen sharing sessions.

2. Fintesk Affiliate Sub-processors​

The Fintesk group does not have affiliate sub-processors.

3. Data Hosting Locations​

Fintesk currently hosts its data in the following AWS regions:

  • US East
  • US West

Data Center Assignment​

To optimize performance and compliance, new sign-ups are primarily hosted in:

  • USA

Note: Due to external conditions (such as IP mappings), company accounts can be hosted in data centers different from the default ones. To confirm the exact location of your company's data, please contact the Support team.
