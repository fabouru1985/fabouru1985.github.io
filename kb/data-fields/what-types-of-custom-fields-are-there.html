What types of custom fields are there?

Custom fields can be used when the information you want to add to Fintesk doesn’t exist as a default field.

tip

Note: The number of custom fields you can have is determined by the usage limits on your plan, which you can learn more about in this article.

For example, there is no default field for “job title,” but you can include that information using a single option or text type custom field under person (contacts).

Types of custom fields​

There are fourteen (at the moment) different types of custom fields, with different kinds of information you can add to your deals, persons, organizations, or products.

Field nameWhat it’s forCommon uses
TextNotes, URLs or other textSecondary email addresses, (short) comments, job titles
Large textLonger notesLonger comments, descriptions
Single optionList of predefined options where only one can be selected (Maximum 500 options)Category, industry, job title
Multiple optionsList of predefined options where multiple items can be selected (Maximum 500 options)Industry, region, competitors, job title, item status
NumericalNumerical dataPriority level, numbered ranking, units requested
MonetaryNumerical data prefixed with a currency symbol.Commission, estimates, costs
UserTag a Fintesk user from your Fintesk accountPrevious or secondary deal or contact owners, users related to the larger project
OrganizationTag an organization from your Fintesk database.Related or secondary parties, partner organizations, holding companies
PersonTag a person from your Fintesk database.Related or secondary person contact
PhoneEnter a phone numberSecondary Skype names, secondary phone numbers, primary phone number for linked item (deal, org.)
TimeSpecific time chosen from a time drop-down. Time format will match your default time zone format.Delivery time, lunchtime
Time RangeRange of time chosen from a time drop-down. Time format will match your default time zone format.Office hours, best time to contact
DateSpecific date chosen from a drop-down calendarDelivery date, deadlines
Date RangeRange of dates chosen from a drop-down calendarEvent dates, completion estimates, holidays

Things to keep in mind​

  • User, organization, and person-type custom fields will click through to the item entered in those values.
  • Persons in Fintesk already have a default phone field, but a phone-type custom field can be added under other items (organizations, deals, etc.).
  • Organizations in Fintesk already have a default address field, but an address-type custom field can be added under other items (deals, persons, etc.)
  • When adding a value in a person or organization field, if a contact with that name doesn’t already exist in your account, a new one will be created
  • The currency of a monetary field will match your default currency setting unless otherwise specified.
